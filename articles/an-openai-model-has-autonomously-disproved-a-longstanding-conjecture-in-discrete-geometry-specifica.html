An OpenAI model has autonomously disproved a longstanding conjecture in discrete geometry, specifically the planar unit distance problem posed by Erdő

📎 Original Source:

Summary

An OpenAI model has autonomously disproved a longstanding conjecture in discrete geometry, specifically the planar unit distance problem posed by Erdős in 1946, by constructing point configurations with more unit-distance pairs than previously believed possible. The proof, which leverages sophisticated algebraic number theory, marks a milestone for both mathematics and AI, demonstrating that AI systems can generate original, significant mathematical insights.
