The article explains that strong AI models alone do not guarantee reliable execution; failures often

📎 Original Source:
https://walkinglabs.github.io/learn-harness-engineering/en/lectures/lecture-01-why-capable-agents-still-fail/

Summary

The article explains that strong AI models alone do not guarantee reliable execution; failures often stem from deficiencies in the engineering harness—such as unclear task specifications, missing verification, and poor environment setup—rather than the model itself. It advocates for harness engineering practices like explicit definitions of done, project documentation, and diagnostic loops to systematically improve agent reliability.
