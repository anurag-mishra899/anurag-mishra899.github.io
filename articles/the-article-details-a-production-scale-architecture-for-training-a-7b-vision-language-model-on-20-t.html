The article details a production-scale architecture for training a 7B vision-language model on 20+ TB of multimodal data using 160 GPUs, leveraging Mo

Web Article 📅 2026-03-01 👤 Shared by Anurag Mishra 🏷️ LLM Training & Fine-tuning
📎 Original Source:
https://krish-mahajan.github.io/blog/streaming-architecture/

Summary

The article details a production-scale architecture for training a 7B vision-language model on 20+ TB of multimodal data using 160 GPUs, leveraging MosaicML Streaming (MDS) to efficiently stream and cache data from cloud storage without downloading the entire dataset. It explains how MDS overcomes the limitations of naive data loading by using shard-based caching, prefetching, and a global index for fault-tolerant, high-throughput training across decoupled pipelines.
