Summary

The article discusses the emergence of "harness engineering" as a critical architectural layer above context and prompt engineering for AI agents, emphasizing that the surrounding system (the harness) is more important than the underlying model. It details LangChain's Deep Agents SDK, which codifies four key primitives—detailed prompts, planning tools, subagents, and filesystems—and demonstrates significant performance gains by optimizing the agent harness rather than the model itself.
