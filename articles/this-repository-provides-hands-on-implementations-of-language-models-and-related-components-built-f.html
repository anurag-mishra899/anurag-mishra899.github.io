Summary

This repository provides hands-on implementations of language models and related components, built from first principles to facilitate learning and understanding of transformers. It includes step-by-step projects such as character-level GPT, GPT-2 reproduction and improvements, BPE tokenizers, LLM inference, supervised fine-tuning, expert iteration, and GRPO, with detailed experiments and ablation studies.
