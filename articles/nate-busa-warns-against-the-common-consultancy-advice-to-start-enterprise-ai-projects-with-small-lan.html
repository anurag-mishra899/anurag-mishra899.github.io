Nate Busa warns against the common consultancy advice to start enterprise AI projects with Small Language Models (SLMs), arguing that SLMs often fail

Summary

Nate Busa warns against the common consultancy advice to start enterprise AI projects with Small Language Models (SLMs), arguing that SLMs often fail in complex, agentic workflows and lead to costly project delays or failures. He recommends deploying the largest, most advanced models feasible from the outset for greater robustness, reliability, and long-term value in enterprise environments.
