NVIDIA has introduced the Agent Toolkit, an open-source platform designed to help enterprises build, deploy, and manage autonomous AI agents with enha

📎 Original Source:

Summary

NVIDIA has introduced the Agent Toolkit, an open-source platform designed to help enterprises build, deploy, and manage autonomous AI agents with enhanced safety, security, and efficiency. The toolkit includes components like OpenShell for secure runtime, AI-Q Blueprint for agentic search, and Nemotron open models, and is being adopted by major enterprise software providers to power advanced, context-aware AI agents across various industries. Partnerships with companies such as Adobe, Atlassian, Cisco, Salesforce, and others highlight the toolkit's broad impact on the future of knowledge work and enterprise automation.
