Summary

Stanford CS149, Fall 2025, is a course focused on parallel computing, covering fundamental principles, engineering trade-offs, and programming techniques for modern parallel systems. The curriculum includes both hardware and software aspects, with lectures, assignments, and case studies on CPUs, GPUs, AI accelerators, and specialized hardware.
