Summary

The article discusses the limitations of naive GraphRAG implementations for knowledge extraction, highlighting issues like duplicate entities, inconsistencies, and missing data when applied to real-world medical records. It introduces the concept of an Ontology Operating System as a solution to build a self-improving knowledge graph that not only stores data but also understands, validates, and evolves it.
