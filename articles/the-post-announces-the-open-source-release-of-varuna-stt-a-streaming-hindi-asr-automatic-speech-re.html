Summary

The post announces the open-source release of Varuna STT, a streaming Hindi ASR (Automatic Speech Recognition) model based on NVIDIA's Nemotron architecture, specifically designed for real-time voice agents and telephony applications. Varuna STT addresses common industry challenges such as low-latency streaming, in-model inverse text normalization (ITN), and self-hostability, offering an MIT-licensed alternative to proprietary or English-only solutions.
