The post announces the open-source release of Varuna STT, a streaming Hindi ASR (Automatic Speech Recognition) model based on NVIDIA's Nemotron archit

Summary

The post announces the open-source release of Varuna STT, a streaming Hindi ASR (Automatic Speech Recognition) model based on NVIDIA's Nemotron architecture and optimized for real-time, low-latency telephony and voice agent applications. The model addresses common challenges faced by Hindi voice-agent teams, such as lack of domain-tuning, high latency, and poor unit economics with commercial APIs, by providing a self-hostable, MIT-licensed solution with built-in inverse text normalization (ITN) and efficient GPU utilization.
