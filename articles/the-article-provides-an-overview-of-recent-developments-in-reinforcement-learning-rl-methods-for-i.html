The article provides an overview of recent developments in reinforcement learning (RL) methods for improving reasoning in large language models (LLMs)

Summary

The article provides an overview of recent developments in reinforcement learning (RL) methods for improving reasoning in large language models (LLMs), tracing the evolution from foundational algorithms like REINFORCE and PPO to newer refinements such as GRPO. It explains the RL setup for LLMs, the challenges of reward assignment, and the practical adaptations of RL algorithms for language model fine-tuning.
