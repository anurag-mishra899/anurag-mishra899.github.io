Sarvam AI has open-sourced two large language models, Sarvam 30B and Sarvam 105B, both designed for advanced reasoning and optimized for efficient dep

📎 Original Source:

Summary

Sarvam AI has open-sourced two large language models, Sarvam 30B and Sarvam 105B, both designed for advanced reasoning and optimized for efficient deployment across diverse hardware. Developed entirely in India, these models feature a Mixture-of-Experts Transformer architecture, extensive multilingual and domain-specific training data, and robust supervised and reinforcement learning pipelines, achieving state-of-the-art performance on Indian language benchmarks.
