Summary

The article details the development of Delphi, an open scaling suite by the Marin team, which provides a robust scaling recipe, a suite of trained models, and a scaling law capable of accurately predicting model performance up to 300× beyond the largest training run. Delphi's approach enables empirical, reproducible scaling studies by releasing all checkpoints, recipes, and data mixtures, and demonstrates that with proper recipe adjustments, small-scale experiments can reliably forecast large-scale model outcomes.
