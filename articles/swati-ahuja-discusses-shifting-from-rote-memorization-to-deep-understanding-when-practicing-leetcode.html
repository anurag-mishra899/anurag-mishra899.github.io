Summary

Swati Ahuja discusses shifting from rote memorization to deep understanding when practicing LeetCode problems, emphasizing the importance of recognizing underlying patterns and the reasoning behind algorithmic techniques rather than just recalling solutions. She advocates for studying solutions first to reverse engineer the logic, mapping problem signals to specific patterns, and provides a resource sheet of these patterns to help others improve their problem-solving skills.
