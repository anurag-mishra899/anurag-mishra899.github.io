Summary

The post discusses benchmarking different H100 GPU instance types (SXM, PCIe, NVL) on Runpod for training the Nanochat d26 model, inspired by Andrej Karpathy's comment that "Nanochat is the new MNIST." The author finds that although SXM is more expensive per hour, it is the most cost-effective and fastest for training, provided NUMA node configurations are managed properly.
