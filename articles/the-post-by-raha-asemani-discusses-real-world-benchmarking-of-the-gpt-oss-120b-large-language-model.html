Summary

The post by Raha Asemani discusses real-world benchmarking of the GPT-OSS-120B large language model (LLM) in production environments, highlighting how typical benchmarks often miss practical failure points under load. Using KServe with vLLM on a single NVIDIA H100 GPU, the team found that system performance degrades and saturates at specific concurrency thresholds, with many benchmark failures attributed to load-generator instability rather than model limits.
