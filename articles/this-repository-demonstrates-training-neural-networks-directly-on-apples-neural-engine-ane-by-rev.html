Summary

This repository demonstrates training neural networks directly on Apple's Neural Engine (ANE) by reverse-engineering private APIs, bypassing Apple's inference-only restrictions. It provides proof-of-concept code, benchmarks, and a from-scratch transformer training implementation, highlighting both the capabilities and current limitations of ANE for training tasks. The project is intended for research and exploration rather than production use.
