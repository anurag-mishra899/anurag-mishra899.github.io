The article explains that strong AI models often fail in real-world coding tasks not due to model limitations, but because of inadequate engineering i

Summary

The article explains that strong AI models often fail in real-world coding tasks not due to model limitations, but because of inadequate engineering infrastructure ("harness"). It emphasizes diagnosing and improving the harness—task specification, environment, verification, and state management—before upgrading models, using practical steps like explicit completion criteria and project documentation.
