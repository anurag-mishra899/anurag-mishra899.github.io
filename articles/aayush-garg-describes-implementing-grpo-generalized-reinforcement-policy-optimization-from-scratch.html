Summary

Aayush Garg describes implementing GRPO (Generalized Reinforcement Policy Optimization) from scratch, focusing on training the Qwen 2.5-Math-1.5B model using pure reinforcement learning and conducting extensive ablation studies to understand the impact of different training choices. He shares insights on experiment setup, optimizations for running on a single RTX 4090 GPU, and cost-effective parallel runs using Modal, with all code and logs made publicly available.
