Summary

The post discusses real-world benchmarking of the GPT-OSS-120B large language model in production environments, highlighting how true system limits and failure points differ from synthetic lab benchmarks. Using KServe, vLLM, and a single NVIDIA H100 GPU, the team observed specific concurrency thresholds where latency, throughput, and success rates degrade, emphasizing that many benchmark failures stem from load generator instability rather than model limitations.
