Summary

The article introduces Spec Kit, an open source toolkit designed to enable spec-driven development with AI coding agents like GitHub Copilot, Claude Code, and Gemini CLI. It outlines a structured four-phase process—specify, plan, tasks, implement—where specifications serve as living, executable artifacts that guide AI agents to generate, test, and validate code, resulting in higher quality and more reliable software. The approach addresses the limitations of vague AI prompting by making specifications the central source of truth throughout the development workflow.
