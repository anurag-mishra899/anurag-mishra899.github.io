Summary

The article discusses continual learning in AI agents, highlighting that learning can occur at three layers: the model (weights), the harness (agent code and tools), and the context (external instructions and skills). It explains how each layer can be updated over time, the challenges involved, and the importance of execution traces in driving improvements across these layers.
