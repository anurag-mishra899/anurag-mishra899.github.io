Nate Busa warns against the common consultancy advice to start enterprise AI projects with small lan

Summary

Nate Busa warns against the common consultancy advice to start enterprise AI projects with small language models (SLMs), arguing that SLMs often fail in complex, agentic workflows and lead to costly delays and mediocre results. He recommends deploying the largest, most robust models feasible from the outset to ensure reliability, stability, and real enterprise value, highlighting the minimal cost difference compared to the risks of failed deployments.
