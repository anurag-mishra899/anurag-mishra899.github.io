Summary

Mohammed Arsalan implemented Google's TurboQuant research paper on KV cache compression for large language models, rebuilding it from scratch in pure PyTorch on a Tesla T4 GPU without custom CUDA kernels. His approach achieved a 4.74x memory reduction and a 12% speed improvement in token generation while maintaining generation quality, demonstrating significant efficiency gains for running LLMs on limited hardware.
