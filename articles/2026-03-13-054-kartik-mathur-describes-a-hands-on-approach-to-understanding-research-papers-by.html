Summary

Kartik Mathur describes a hands-on approach to understanding research papers by first implementing their core algorithms using Claude Code, rather than starting with theoretical proofs. This "build first, read later" method involves creating simplified, observable prototypes to clarify key mechanisms before revisiting the paper's math, making the research more concrete and debuggable. He also shares prompt templates for replicating this workflow and discusses community feedback on the approach.
