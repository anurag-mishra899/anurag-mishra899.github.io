Summary

Sarvam AI has open-sourced two large language models, Sarvam 30B and Sarvam 105B, designed for advanced reasoning and efficient deployment, especially on Indian languages and hardware. The models were developed entirely in India, featuring a Mixture-of-Experts Transformer architecture, custom training pipelines, and strong performance across benchmarks, with open access to weights and APIs.
