Summary

The article details the process of provisioning, networking, and orchestrating a 160-GPU training cluster on AWS using 20 p5.48xlarge nodes, focusing on the infrastructure setup required before distributed training can begin. It discusses the choice of AWS Batch as the orchestrator due to hardware availability, the complexities of configuring networking (especially EFA and NCCL), and the construction of a Docker-based GPU-ready environment.
