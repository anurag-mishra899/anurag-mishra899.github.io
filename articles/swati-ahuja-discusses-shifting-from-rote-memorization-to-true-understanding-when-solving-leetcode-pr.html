Summary

Swati Ahuja discusses shifting from rote memorization to true understanding when solving LeetCode problems by focusing on recognizing and internalizing underlying problem patterns rather than just recalling solutions. She advocates for studying solutions first to identify the "why" behind each technique, mapping problem signals to specific patterns, and emphasizes that deeper understanding—not higher problem count—leads to real skill improvement.
