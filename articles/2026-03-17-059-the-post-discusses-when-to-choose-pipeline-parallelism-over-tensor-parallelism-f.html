Summary

The post discusses when to choose pipeline parallelism over tensor parallelism for running large language models (LLMs) like 70B parameter models, emphasizing that the choice depends on GPU interconnect speed and deployment architecture. Pipeline parallelism is preferred across multiple nodes with slower interconnects, while tensor parallelism suits single-node setups with fast links like NVLink; in practice, a hybrid approach is often used to balance throughput and latency.
