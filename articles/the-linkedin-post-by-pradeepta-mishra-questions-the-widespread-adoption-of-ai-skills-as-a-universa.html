Summary

The LinkedIn post by Pradeepta Mishra questions the widespread adoption of "AI Skills" as a universal solution, arguing that while they improve consistency for repeatable tasks, they can limit adaptability in complex, context-driven work. The discussion emphasizes the importance of foundational models, clear context, and human oversight, suggesting that AI should augment rather than oversimplify dynamic problem-solving.
