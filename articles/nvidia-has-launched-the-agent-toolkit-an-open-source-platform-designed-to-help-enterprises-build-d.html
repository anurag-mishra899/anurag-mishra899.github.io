NVIDIA has launched the Agent Toolkit, an open-source platform designed to help enterprises build, deploy, and manage autonomous AI agents with enhanc

Summary

NVIDIA has launched the Agent Toolkit, an open-source platform designed to help enterprises build, deploy, and manage autonomous AI agents with enhanced safety, security, and efficiency. The toolkit includes components like OpenShell for secure runtime, Nemotron open models, and the AI-Q Blueprint, and is being adopted by major software companies to power next-generation AI-driven workflows and applications. Collaborations with partners such as LangChain, Cisco, Adobe, Salesforce, and others highlight its broad industry impact.
